Street2Simulator — Bicycle IMU Dataset

Fraunhofer IAIS · Comparative inertial measurement dataset for real bicycle rides and simulated equivalents

About the Dataset

Street2Simulator is a comparative dataset pairing real-world bicycle rides with physics-based simulated counterparts. Each participant rode a predefined urban route while wearing six body-mounted IMU sensors recording 3-axis acceleration and gyroscope data at high frequency. The same route was then reproduced in simulation, enabling direct comparison of biomechanical signals between real and simulated conditions.

The dataset comprises 10 participant pairs, with sensors placed at six body positions:

PHR — Head RKL — Body RHL — Hand Left RBL — Hand Right PVL — Leg Left RBR — Leg Right
